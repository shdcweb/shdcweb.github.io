Counseling


Counseling aims at enhancing the psychological well being of the child. It involves interactions of special child with a professionally trained therapist who facilitates this process of self understanding and inner growth. The counseling process is unique to each child and may involve suggestion of techniques and alternate behaviors.
Areas covered under counselling:

➜ Individual counseling
➜ Family counseling
➜ Marital/Couple counseling
➜ Group counseling
➜ Behavior modification
➜ Awareness workshops
